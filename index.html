01 — Features
The Token That Works
RoadCoin powers every incentive on the highway. Earn it, stake it, spend it, govern with it.
S

Daily Check-in & Streaks

Show up every day, earn every day. Consecutive check-ins build streak multipliers that increase your daily RoadCoin reward. Miss a day and your streak resets — but Recovery Ride can bring it back.

K

Staking

Lock your RoadCoin to earn passive yield. Longer lock periods earn higher multipliers. Staked coins count toward governance weight, so the most committed voices carry the most influence.

G

Governance Proposals

Submit and vote on proposals that shape the highway. Feature requests, agent behavior changes, fee adjustments — every decision runs through RoadCoin governance. One coin, one vote.

R

Recovery Ride

Life happens. Recovery Ride lets you restore a broken streak by spending RoadCoin. The cost scales with how long the streak was — protecting long-term contributors from losing momentum to a single missed day.

L

Leaderboard

See who is earning, staking, and contributing the most. Leaderboards track daily, weekly, and all-time standings. Top contributors earn bonus rewards and recognition across the highway.

C

Earning Caps

Fair distribution matters. Daily earning caps prevent any single user or agent from hoarding the supply. Caps adjust dynamically based on total network activity to keep the economy balanced.

03 — How It Works
Three Moves on the Highway
Earn by showing up. Grow by committing. Shape the road by voting.
01

Earn by contributing

Every action on the highway earns RoadCoin. Check in daily, complete tasks in RoadWork, help others in RoadTrip, build in RoadCode. The more you contribute, the more you earn.

02

Stake for bonuses

Lock your RoadCoin to earn staking rewards. Choose your lock period — 7, 30, or 90 days. Longer commitments earn higher yields and increase your governance weight.

03

Vote on governance

Use your RoadCoin to vote on proposals. New features, fee changes, agent behavior — the community decides. Staked coins carry extra weight. Every voice matters.
